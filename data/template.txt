================================================================================
POST-PROCESSING EFFECTS (applied after generation, not prompted)
================================================================================

Effect              | Probability | Parameters
--------------------|-------------|------------------------------------------
JPEG compression    | 30%         | quality: uniform [15, 85]
Chroma subsampling  | 25%         | downsample Cb/Cr 2x, then bilinear upsample
Gaussian noise      | 20%         | sigma: uniform [5, 25], apply to RGB only
Depth-of-field blur | 15%         | gaussian blur on alpha edges, sigma [1, 5px]
Motion blur         | 10%         | directional kernel, angle from action, len [3, 15px]
Interlacing         | 5%          | split even/odd scanlines, 1-frame offset

Notes:
- Each effect is an independent coin flip (they stack)
- Apply in order: noise -> chroma subsample -> DOF blur -> motion blur -> JPEG
- Compression goes last so it interacts with other artifacts (realistic)
- Motion blur should be applied to BOTH the RGB and alpha channels
- DOF blur should primarily target the alpha channel edges
- For chroma subsampling: convert to YCbCr, downsample Cb and Cr by 2x
  in both dimensions, then upsample back with bilinear interpolation
